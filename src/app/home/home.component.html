Hi, I’m a 3D artist

I focus on environment design and props. I’m passionate about creating immersive worlds. I work primarily in Maya, Substance, Photoshop, & Unreal Engine and I’m always pushing to level up my skills and bring more emotion and meaning into the scenes I create. I thrive in a busy, creative, and collaborative space.

I love turning
rough ideas into visually stunning environments.
